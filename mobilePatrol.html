Why Wimmera Mobile Patrol ?

At Wimmera Security Service, our mobile patrol services deliver unparalleled protection for homes, businesses, and events across Horsham and the Wimmera Region. Leveraging cutting-edge technology and over 30 years of expertise, we provide a proactive, efficient, and transparent security solution tailored to your needs. Discover why we’re the trusted choice for mobile security patrols in Australia.

  • Advanced Technology Integration: Our mobile patrols utilize state-of-the-art GPS tracking, real-time incident reporting, and cloud-based systems to ensure accountability and visibility. Stay informed with instant updates through our secure client portal.
  • Real-Time Client Portal: Access live data on patrol routes, incident reports, and guard activities. Our portal provides detailed logs, GPS-verified checkpoints, and high-resolution images for complete transparency.
  • Instant Incident Reporting: In the event of an incident, our patrol officers generate comprehensive reports with photos, detailed descriptions, and actions taken, automatically delivered to you via our cloud-based platform.
  • Proactive Risk Reduction: Our team’s continuous training and innovative strategies anticipate security trends, minimizing risks and ensuring your property remains secure.
  • Customized Patrol Schedules: We design flexible patrol plans to suit your specific requirements, whether for a business, residence, or event, providing 24/7 security patrols you can rely on.
  • Community-Focused Expertise: With over three decades serving the Wimmera Region, our passionate and professional team is dedicated to protecting our community with respect and commitment.
